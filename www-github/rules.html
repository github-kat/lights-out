rules

Clicking on a window toggles its light and each light adjacent to it. Turn off all the lights to get the party started!

An example of how the lights toggle

Try turning off all lights using the fewest amount of clicks. There is a hint and a solution button for when you are stuck. The hint is enabled after 10 moves and the solution after 15 moves. You can reset the grid to view what it was initially.

Each layout has a solution. Some more difficult than others. The level of difficulty is chosen at random.
